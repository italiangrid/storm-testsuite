##### METHODS

  ${url}  Build FileTransfer URL  ${endpoint}  ${storageArea}/${relativePath}
  ${output}  Curl  GET  ${url}  ${credentials} -iv
  [Return]  ${output}

  ${output}  Curl  PUT  ${url}  -T ${localFilePath} -iv ${credentials}

  ${url}  Build FileTransfer URL  ${endpoint}  ${storageArea}/${relativePath}
  ${output}  Curl  PUT  ${url}  ${body} -iv ${credentials}
  [Return]  ${output}
